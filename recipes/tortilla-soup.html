Tortilla Soup

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Tortilla
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes, the onions and the garlic
  2. Roast the veggies
  3. Boil some watter
  4. Put the chicken on the water until cooked
  5. Take out the chicken to shred while you use the chicken stock to make soup with the veggies and tortilla
  6. Reduce for 20 min and serve with shreded Chicken

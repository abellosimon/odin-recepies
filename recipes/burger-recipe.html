Burger

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes and the onions
  2. Season and cook the meat
  3. Warm up the bread buns
  4. When the meat is almost done, add some cheese for it to melt
  5. Put come ketchup on the buns
  6. Assemble!
